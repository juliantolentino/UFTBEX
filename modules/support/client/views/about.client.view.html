About Us and the issle project

Who are we?

We are a collection of students at the University of Florida who were approached to design a website for Software Engineering (CEN3031). The need was for a tool to allows teachers in the state of Florida to create STEM lesson plans easily. This website is a practical way for us to give back to the education system that we grew up in.

Why the Engineering Design Process?

It has been observed that most students have difficulty distinguishing between major concepts, main ideas, and details. By having students apply a systematic engineering approach to real problems they will learn to distinguish between essential-to-know information and trivia.

How is STEM integrated?

By searching through our database of Florida standards you can find relevant standards to the engineering projects you're creating for your class. Not only will this allow you to meet the state requirements, it also allows for the integration of previously separate subjects.
